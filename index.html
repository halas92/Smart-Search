About this App

About this App

Smart Search is a platform that connects journalism graduate students quickly and simply with available job and internships based on user interests, qualifications and desired location. Receive personalized job recommendations, reduce your anxiety and save time finding a job with Smart Search.

Recommended Jobs

View personalized job and internship suggestions based on the filters you selected when creating your profile - get immediate satisfaction.

Saved Jobs

Find an organized list of all the jobs you swiped right on. Don’t waste any time - job applications have never been easier.

Passed Jobs

Swiped left by accident? Don’t worry, those jobs aren’t gone forever. Review positions you rejected on the Passed Jobs page and get a second chance to apply.

Deadlines

Trouble remembering deadlines? We make it easy to keep track of application due dates so you don’t miss the opportunity apply for your dream job.

Create Your Profile

Market yourself to potential employers. Write a short bio of who you are and what you can offer. Upload your resume and photo and add skills to your profile page for optimal visibility.

Search

Know what you’re looking for? Use the search function to quickly navigate the recommended jobs and find your perfect fit.

A job search, made for you.

Sign-Up

Using your email account, select a username and password for easy login.

Upload

Create your profile by uploading a picture and resume. Tell employers more about yourself by including a short bio.

Select your interests and location

Find jobs near you by setting a location when creating your profile. Choose up to three journalism categories that interest you the most for optimized job recommendations.

View recommended jobs

Have fun swiping through job recommendations carefully selected just for you! Each recommendation page is complete with the job title, location, summary and application deadline. Accept or reject suggestions to keep viewing jobs.
